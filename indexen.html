Hi! We’re Lauro and Martín, co-founders of Latin Merch.

Based in Buenos Aires, we are passionate entrepreneurs.

In our creative workshop, every corporate gift begins with a conversation and is carefully crafted. We seek that special detail that connects your brand with its recipients, leaving a genuine impression. From concept to delivery, our goal is for your gift to speak for your company and create lasting connections.

Some of our most requested products are:

  • Keychains and phone straps.
  • Onboarding kit for companies.
  • Branded towels.

We offer highly flexible working arrangements, competitive prices, and affordable options tailored for large companies, SMEs, and entrepreneurs alike. Write to us, and together we’ll create a personalized quote with no obligation.
