Over 5 years creating quality corporate gifts

Hi! We are Lauro and Martín, co-founders of Latin Merch. From Buenos Aires Capital, we are passionate entrepreneurs.

In our idea workshop, every corporate gift is born from a conversation and carefully crafted. We look for that special detail that connects your brand with those who receive it, leaving a genuine impression. From conception to delivery, our goal is that your gift speaks for your company and creates lasting bonds.

Some of our most requested products are:

  • Keychains and phone straps
  • Onboarding kits for companies
  • Custom towels

We offer highly flexible working arrangements, competitive prices and accessible options, adapted for large companies, SMEs and entrepreneurs. Write to us and we will prepare a personalized budget together, with no obligation.
